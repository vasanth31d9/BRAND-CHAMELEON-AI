# Brand Chameleon AI - Features

## 🎯 Core Features

### 1. Multi-Format Content Input
- **Text Input**: Paste your ideas directly
- **URL Scraping**: Extract content from any webpage
- **PDF Upload**: Upload documents and extract key insights
- **Voice Recording**: Record voice notes and transcribe automatically

### 2. Brand Voice DNA Engine
- Upload 10-20 of your past posts
- AI analyzes your unique writing style
- Learns tone, vocabulary, and sentence structure
- Stores embeddings in Pinecone vector database
- Maintains consistency across all generated content

### 3. Multi-Platform Content Generation
Generate optimized content for:
- **LinkedIn**: Professional posts with insights
- **Twitter/X**: Engaging threads with hooks
- **Instagram**: Visual captions with emojis and hashtags
- **TikTok**: Video scripts with transitions
- **Blog Articles**: Long-form content with SEO
- **YouTube**: Video scripts with intro/outro

### 4. Trend Injection
- Real-time trending topics from Twitter/X
- Hot discussions from Reddit
- Google Trends integration
- Keyword suggestions for better reach
- Platform-specific trend analysis

### 5. Virality Prediction Engine
Each post gets scored on:
- **Hook Strength** (0-10): Opening line effectiveness
- **Readability** (0-10): Ease of reading
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Positive/Negative/Neutral
- **SEO Score** (0-10): Search optimization
- **Engagement Potential**: Predicted likes/shares
- **Overall Virality Score** (0-10)

### 6. Smart Scheduler
- AI suggests optimal posting times
- Platform-specific best times
- Auto-schedule entire content queue
- Integration with social media APIs
- Timezone-aware scheduling

### 7. AI Media Generation
- Generate images for posts (DALL-E)
- Create thumbnails automatically
- Video script generation
- Reel and Short captions
- Alt text for accessibility

### 8. Multilingual Support
Generate content in:
- English
- Hindi (हिंदी)
- Telugu (తెలుగు)
- Tamil (தமிழ்)
- Bengali (বাংলা)

### 9. Analytics Dashboard
Track performance metrics:
- Total posts published
- Engagement rates by platform
- Average virality scores
- Reach and impressions
- Best performing content
- Platform comparison charts
- Growth trends over time

### 10. Team Collaboration
- Shared workspace for teams
- Role-based permissions (Admin/Editor/Viewer)
- Comment on drafts
- Approval workflows
- Activity tracking
